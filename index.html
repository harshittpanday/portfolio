About Me

I am a self-driven student developer with a strong interest in building practical, real-world software solutions. My focus lies in web development, application design, and exploring how modern tools like AI can be used to create meaningful and efficient systems.
Over time, I have worked on multiple personal projects including real-time chat applications, AI-based study tools, and game prototypes. These projects have helped me strengthen my understanding of both frontend and backend development, along with improving my problem-solving and system design skills.
I enjoy turning ideas into working products rather than just theoretical concepts. This approach has helped me learn how to structure applications, manage data flow, debug complex issues, and continuously improve performance and user experience.

I am particularly interested in:

* AI-assisted tools and automation

Currently, I am focused on improving my full-stack development skills and working on more advanced projects that involve real-time systems and intelligent features.
My long-term goal is to grow as a software developer who can design and build impactful digital products that solve real problems and provide value to users.
